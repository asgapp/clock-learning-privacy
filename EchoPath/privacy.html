Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Welcome to EchoPath ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application EchoPath (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

EchoPath is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the App.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect the following information automatically:

  • Game Progress: Your level completion status, star ratings, and best completion times are stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase transactions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Track your game progress and achievements
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage

Your game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync your data (if such feature is available in future updates).

4. In-App Purchases

When you make an in-app purchase:

  • All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device to unlock content
  • You can restore purchases on any device using the same Apple ID

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For in-app purchases and app distribution
  • Apple Analytics: For app analytics and crash reporting (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

EchoPath is suitable for users of all ages.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your game data stored locally on your device
  • Delete the App and all associated data by uninstalling it
