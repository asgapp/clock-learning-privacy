Getting Started

Welcome to EchoPath! Here's how to get started:

  1. Launch the app and you'll see the level selection screen
  2. Start with Level 1 - it's always unlocked and free to play
  3. Watch the path preview carefully - you'll have 1 second to memorize it
  4. Trace the path from the green square to the red square
  5. Complete levels to unlock the next ones sequentially

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play EchoPath?
A: Watch the path appear on the grid for 1 second, then trace it from the green starting point to the red ending point using your finger. You must follow the exact path you saw during the preview.
Q: How many levels are there?
A: EchoPath has 200 unique levels. The first 6 levels are free to play with unlimited attempts. Levels 7-200 require a one-time in-app purchase to unlock.
Q: What are the star ratings?
A: You can earn 1-3 stars based on your completion time:
  • ⭐ 3 Stars: Fastest completion time
  • ⭐ 2 Stars: Good completion time
  • ⭐ 1 Star: Acceptable completion time
Q: What is the lives system?
A: Levels 7+ use a lives system. You start with 5 lives per level. If you fail a level, you lose one life. Lives automatically refill after 10 minutes when you run out. Levels 1-6 have unlimited lives.
Q: How do I unlock more levels?
A: Complete levels sequentially to unlock the next one. To unlock levels 7-200, you'll need to purchase the full game unlock through an in-app purchase.
Q: How do I restore my purchase?
A: If you've previously purchased the full game unlock, tap "Restore Purchases" on the purchase screen. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.
Q: My progress was lost. How do I recover it?
A: Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstalled and reinstalled the app, your progress may be lost. However, your purchase can be restored using "Restore Purchases" if you're signed in with the same Apple ID.
Q: The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
  4. Update the app to the latest version from the App Store
  5. If the problem persists, contact support
Q: Can I play on multiple devices?
A: Your purchase can be restored on any device using the same Apple ID. However, game progress is stored locally on each device and does not sync between devices.

Tips & Tricks

  • Focus on the path: Pay close attention during the preview - you only have 1 second!
  • Start from green: Always begin tracing from the green square
  • End at red: Make sure you complete the path by reaching the red square
  • Practice makes perfect: The first 6 levels are free - use them to master the game
  • Speed matters: Complete levels faster to earn more stars
  • Manage your lives: For levels 7+, be careful - you have limited lives!
