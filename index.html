Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Clock Learning is an educational app designed for children to practice and learn to read analog clocks. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in compliance with applicable child privacy laws, including COPPA and GDPR where applicable.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. We do not require users to log in or provide any identifying details.

2. Advertising

Clock Learning displays ads using Google AdMob. We use settings appropriate for child-directed services:

  • All ads are tagged as child-directed and are suitable for children.
  • We use non-personalized ads only.
  • No user profiling or tracking is performed.

For more information, please visit Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use Google AdMob for showing ads. These services may collect limited technical data (e.g., device model, app version) to serve appropriate non-personalized ads. This data is handled in accordance with Google’s policies.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is directed to children under 13. We comply with the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.
